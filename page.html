Who is Steve Jobs?

Steve Jobs is an American business magnate, industrial designer, investor, and media owner. He is the chairman, chief executive (CEO), and co-founder of Apple Inc., chairman and majority shareholder of Pixar, member of the board of directors of The Walt Disney Company following the acquisition of Pixar, and founder, chairman, and CEO of NeXT. Jobs is widely recognized as the pioneer of the personal computer revolution of the 1970s and 1980s, along with Apple co-founder Steve Wozniak.

Timeline

  • 1955: Steven Paul Jobs is born in San Francisco to Joanne Carole Schieble and Abdulfattah Jandali. The then-unmarried couple give up their son to adoption. Paul and Clara Jobs become Jobs' non-biological parents.
  • 1977: Apple incorporates.
  • 1997: Becomes CEO, initially as the de facto chief, then as interim chief in September.
  • 2011: Apple starts selling iPhones via Verizon Wireless, officially ending AT&T's exclusive U.S. contract.
  • 2011: Resigns from Apple CEO post; becomes chairman.
